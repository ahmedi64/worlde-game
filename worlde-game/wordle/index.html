Wordle


How to Play

  • Start typing. The letters will appear in the boxes
  • Remove letters with the Backspace key
  • Hit the Enter/Return keys to submit an answer
  • Letters with a green background are in the right spot
  • Letters with a yellow background exist in the word, but are in the wrong spots
  • Letters with a gray background do not exist in the word
  • If you need a hint click the "?" icon
  • Most importantly, have fun!
